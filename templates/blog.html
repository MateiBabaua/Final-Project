How did I end up in this situation?

  • {{ contact_info[1] }}

  • {{ comments_index }}

I enjoy challenges.

I was a high-performance athlete, raised with a champion's mindset. Since childhood, I've been taught to forsake trivial interests to achieve ambitious goals. I'm accustomed to sustained hard work, making quick and efficient decisions, always finding optimal and unexpected solutions. Due to medical reasons, I was forced to give up my passion for sports, the joy of my life, and choose a new path.

Because I've always pursued what I love, I believed my transition should be based on the same principle! I find the same level of challenge in programming as in sports, in swiftly and efficiently solving problems, and why not, uniquely. It was an easy choice for me, as during my athletic career, I had free time which I often spent with my PC, as I had to live far from family and travel a lot.

Being my best friend, I discovered in it a gateway to information, continually developing myself. On the other hand, while playing various games, I stumbled upon different bugs. After reporting them to developers, my initial thought was, "how could I solve these myself?", prompting me to seek solutions. That was just the beginning. The best is yet to come!

Success is not final, failure is not fatal. It is the courage to continue that counts

I am passionate about various things that involve creativity, technology, and action, my hobbies include software programming, web development, and a strong interest in cars and driving. These hobbies fuel my love for creating and working with technology while allowing me to explore the exciting world of vehicles.
